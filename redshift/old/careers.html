Careers

We encourage applications from people who can combine imaginative play with technical excellence, and are excited to solve the world's most difficult problems. Simply answer three questions from the list below and send as a pdf (of at most 10 pages), along with a CV, to careers@redshiftlabs.ai.


  • You have a pile of warm metal shavings in the shape of a cone. Discuss.

  • How can a two-level quantum system be used as a gyroscope?

  • In the sewer beneath the Prague Orloj is a locked box. What is inside?

  • Intelligent life evolves from topologically robust quantum matter. Describe its social structure and language.

  • Someone gives you a hot rock. Turn it into a compiler.

  • You control a Dyson sphere around a Kerr black hole. A cat falls in. Outline—explicitly—how to reconstruct its mass, angle and time of infall from gravitational waves impinging on the sphere.

  • What do you find most interesting about the number \(17\)?

  • What is time? What has it got to do with thermodynamics? Why is it one-dimensional?

  • George Box said "All models are wrong, but some are useful." Is this a model?
